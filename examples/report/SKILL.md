# Research report

Write a credible, well-sourced report on a given **topic** for a given **audience**.

## Steps
1. **Research** the topic for that audience — gather recent, credible sources and capture the key facts and tensions.
2. **Outline** the report: an intro, three to five body sections, and a conclusion.
3. **Write** the full report from the outline, citing every source inline, in a tone that fits the audience.
4. **Review** the draft critically — fix weak transitions, unsupported claims, and filler — and produce the final report.

## Standards
- Every claim in the report cites one of the gathered sources.
- The final report reads as finished work, not a draft.
