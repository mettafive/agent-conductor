# Skill: warranty-claim

**Shape:** iterates over each claim in claims.

**Distinct work-units this skill performs** (each is a phase the user will look for on the board):

- **verify coverage** — verify coverage, policy check.
- **assess damage** — assess damage, evaluate.
- **cost estimate** — cost estimate, repair quote.  _(visibility divider — no hard gate needed)_
- **decide claim** — decide, approve deny.

## Procedure
For each claim in claims:
  1. verify coverage — verify coverage, policy check.
  2. assess damage — assess damage, evaluate.
  3. cost estimate — cost estimate, repair quote.
  4. decide claim — decide, approve deny.
