# Skill: market-research

**Shape:** iterates over each competitor in competitors.

**Distinct work-units this skill performs** (each is a phase the user will look for on the board):

- **collect data** — collect, scrape competitor.
- **analyze** — analyze, compare features.
- **pricing table** — pricing table, price compare.  _(visibility divider — no hard gate needed)_
- **swot** — swot, strengths weaknesses.  _(visibility divider — no hard gate needed)_
- **report** — report, writeup.

## Procedure
For each competitor in competitors:
  1. collect data — collect, scrape competitor.
  2. analyze — analyze, compare features.
  3. pricing table — pricing table, price compare.
  4. swot — swot, strengths weaknesses.
  5. report — report, writeup.
