# Skill: trip-itinerary

**Shape:** iterates over each city in cities.

**Distinct work-units this skill performs** (each is a phase the user will look for on the board):

- **research city** — research, attractions.
- **build day plan** — day plan, itinerary.
- **book logistics** — book, transport hotel.  _(visibility divider — no hard gate needed)_
- **budget day** — budget, daily spend.  _(visibility divider — no hard gate needed)_
- **finalize** — finalize, confirm itinerary.

## Procedure
For each city in cities:
  1. research city — research, attractions.
  2. build day plan — day plan, itinerary.
  3. book logistics — book, transport hotel.
  4. budget day — budget, daily spend.
  5. finalize — finalize, confirm itinerary.
